Summary: Latitude vs X

This site is to visualize the weather of 500+ cities across the world of varying distance from the equator. The data is from OpenWeatherMap API.

We analyzed the data with matplotlib and visualized various aspects,like temperature, cloudiness, wind speed and humidity vs latitude.
